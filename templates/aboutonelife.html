What made us work on OneLife and the inspiration behind this?

Each one of us faced issues when it comes to this field. Many of us faced problems from basic Blood infusion to the core Heart Transplantation. All these problems are faced by all sector people, this is the place for High and Low where everyone are equal. The reason behind OneLife is to make work easier for public so they can find the neccessary organ hazel free .

This OneLife project is started by a group of students from Bennett universtiy CSE students. Our main motive to start this project is due to the difficulties that people faced during this pandemic. Due to the high demand of patients and high risk of getting corona, people faced trouble visting for other health issues such as Organ transplant or blood infusion. Considering these issues we have strated this project to help patients and customers to go through a risk free process and a less time consuming task for users.
