Organ Donation Awareness

Donating organs is an important part of our life. Let us spread the awareness of organ donation and blood donation

In The Long Run, Organ Donation Saves Lives

How can we...

You can help us spread awareness about organ donation! Organize a brief awareness session together at your office, college, school, club or anywhere else. Help us get more information about organ donation from your friends , colleagues, family and others.

What india is facing?

India faces an acute shortage of transplant organs. More than a million individuals are estimated to suffer from end-stage organ failure, but only a handful of 3500 transplants are performed annually. At least 15 people die each day while waiting for organs, and a new name is added to this waiting list every 10 minutes. Undoubtedly, the market exceeds the supply of organs by far. And the harsh truth, no one can hide.

How can we?

One way to raise awareness about organ donation is to update your social media profile images with themed banners. Through visiting the Donate Life website, you can find banner links, cover photos, and profile pictures for social media accounts such as Facebook and Twitter. We need to start this awareness at an early age, which lets every person learn about the value of organ donation.

Importance

“Donation is offering an organ and tissue to support someone who wants a transplant. Transplants can save or change a person's life. One organ and one tissue donor will help change the lives of more than 10 people. This depends on donors and their families willing to donate their organs and tissues after death.”

“Three lives can be saved by a single donation. One blood donation includes a range of blood components that can benefit up to three different patients. Blood can not be rendered. Blood can not be made despite medical and technological advancements, but donations are the only way we can provide blood to those who need it.”
